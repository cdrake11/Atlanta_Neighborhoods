Atlanta-Neighborhoods

About

This project explores the Atlanta real estate market in Georgia. We gathered data captured by Atlanta PD crime records, household income, and median listing price data to help visualize what Atlanta has to offer its new residents. If Atlanta fits new residents, click Atlanta Zillow and Atlanta Realter to search for homes.
